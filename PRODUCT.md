# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Prospective clients seeking interior architecture services: affluent homeowners, real estate investors, and businesses needing workspace design. They browse from desktop and mobile, often referred via press or Instagram. They want to see the quality of past work and feel confident in the agency's taste before making contact.

## Product Purpose
Showcase Agence MIND's interior architecture portfolio, communicate Marine Prigent's design philosophy, and convert visitors into client inquiries. Success = a visitor sees 3+ projects and submits the contact form.

## Brand Personality
Minimalist, ultra-modern, precise. The site should feel like walking into a perfectly curated space — nothing superfluous, every detail intentional.

## Anti-references
- Generic agency templates (Squarespace/Wix cookie-cutter layouts)
- Overly decorative sites with heavy gradients and ornate borders
- Trendy/flashy designs with neon colors, 3D blobs, excessive parallax
- Any site that looks "AI-generated" — cream backgrounds, numbered section markers on every section, tiny uppercase eyebrows everywhere

## Design Principles
1. **Let the work speak** — Projects are the hero. The interface recedes; the architecture advances.
2. **Precision is luxury** — Every pixel, every transition, every whitespace gap is deliberate. No defaults.
3. **Restraint over decoration** — Remove until it breaks, then add one thing back.
4. **Motion as material** — Animation isn't decorative; it reveals spatial relationships like architecture itself.
5. **Confidence through silence** — Large empty spaces communicate mastery, not absence.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA. Body text ≥ 4.5:1 contrast. Respect prefers-reduced-motion. All interactive elements keyboard-accessible.
